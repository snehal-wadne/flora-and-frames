Our Story

A brand built from cozy corners, soft visuals, and personal spaces

Flora and Frames began with the idea that room decor should feel curated, expressive, and easy to style.

Why we started

We wanted to make Pinterest-style room decor easier to bring into real life.

Too often, room inspiration looks beautiful online but feels difficult to recreate. Flora and Frames was created to bridge that gap with decor pieces that actually work together.

From wall posters and fairy lights to vines, frames, and custom decor, our goal is to help people style spaces that feel personal, pretty, and realistic for everyday living.

Designed for everyday room glow-ups
